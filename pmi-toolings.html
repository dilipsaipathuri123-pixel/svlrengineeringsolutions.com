• About PMI Toolings

Excellence in Precision Manufacturing Since 2006

We would like to introduce ourselves as Precision manufacturer and Product developer. We serve Engineering, Medical, FMCG, Electronics, Oil and Gas, Industries.

The company PMI Tooling Private Limited was started by a group of enthusiastic professionals who have an excellent academic background coupled with excellent latest industrial exposure including Product and Tooling development, rapid prototyping and reverse engineering both in India and Overseas in 2006.

PMI was initially intended to be a Precision Die and Mould making company manufacturing injection moulds, press tools, jigs, fixtures and die casting dies and has now further diversified its operations into precision machining and pattern making by pumping in additional investment and moving in high precision State Of The Art CNC machines with the latest Control Systems and multi spindle Wickman machines for mass production items.

PMI has already been supplying to both local and to overseas markets, quite a number of Precision production parts and Tooling.
